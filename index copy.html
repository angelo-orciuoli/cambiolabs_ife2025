Team & Student Skill Dashboard: Institute for Entrepreneurship 2025

Welcome to the skills dashboard for the Institute for Entrepreneurship 2025 program. This interactive report tracks the professional growth of students, showing how their skills have developed from the beginning to the end of the fellowship. You can navigate through the tabs to view charts for entire teams or dive into the progress of individual students. Each chart provides a visual snapshot of their abilities, making it easy to see their journey and development over the 5 week period

How to Read This Dashboard

Understanding the Charts

Each chart is a radar graph that provides a visual snapshot of six key skill areas. The six measured areas are:

  • Adaptability & Creativity
  • Problem-Solving
  • EQ & Ethics
  • Collaboration & Communication
  • Tech Aptitude
  • Time Management

The scores are rated on a scale of 1 to 5. These scores should be seen as a reflection of the skills a student demonstrated during the program, rather than a definitive judgment of what they are "good" or "bad" at.

How Scores Are Calculated

There are two types of assessments you can view for each student:

  • Student Self-Assessment: This view is based on the student's own evaluation of their skills, as recorded in self-assessment surveys completed throughout the program.
  • Overall Assessment: This is a more holistic score calculated from a combination of peer feedback, demonstrated abilities in written assignments, and performance during the final pitch presentations.
